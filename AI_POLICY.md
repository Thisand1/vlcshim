# AI Usage Policy

This project allows AI-assisted work, but only with strict quality and
accountability standards.

## Rules For Contributors

- **Disclose AI usage.** State which tool(s) were used and what parts of the
  submission were AI-assisted.
- **Understand your own changes.** If you cannot explain the code or text you
  submit, do not submit it.
- **Human review is mandatory.** AI-generated drafts for issues, discussions,
  and pull requests must be reviewed and edited by a human before posting.
- **No AI-generated media assets.** AI-generated text and code may be accepted
  under this policy; AI-generated art, images, audio, or video are not.
- **Low-quality AI spam is not accepted.** Repeated low-effort submissions may
  be closed or blocked at maintainer discretion.

These requirements apply to outside contributions. Maintainers may use AI
tools internally with their own review standards.

## Respect Maintainer Time

vlcshim is maintained by humans. Every report and pull request is reviewed by
people who must validate correctness, security impact, and maintainability.
Submissions that are unverified, noisy, or clearly not understood by the
author shift unnecessary review burden to maintainers.

## This Project Is Not Anti-AI

AI is used in this repository and is considered a practical engineering tool.
The restrictions above exist to enforce contribution quality, not to ban AI.
The standard is simple: useful, correct, reviewed contributions are welcome.
